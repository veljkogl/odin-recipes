Rice pudding

The perfect dessert

This is one of my favorite sweet delights for any season, but the colder it is outside the better.

It is a relatively international dish, but the version I make is originally Serbian-Turkish "Sutlijas".

Very easy to make, you just need a bit of elbow grase ;).

Key components:

  • Rice: long-grain or whichever one you got
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon:this one is optional
  • Coconut flour

Steps towards deliciousness:

  1. Take 1 cup of rice and wash it (3-4 times) with cold water
  2. Put the rice in a pot and add a 1 and a half cup water
  3. Cook the rice until the water has almost entirely dissipated
  4. Add 1 and a half cups of milk
  5. Once you add the milk, make sure you watch over the pot and keep stirring the rice (this is where the elbow grease comes into play)
  6. Add 3 tablespoons of sugar, 2 tablespoons of coconut flour, and a bit of Cinnamon
  7. Keep stirring - the rice is done when it is not hard but also not falling appart
  8. Add whatever you think the recipe needs :)
