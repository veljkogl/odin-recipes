Rice pudding

The perfect dessert

This is one of my favorite sweet delights for any season, but the colder it is outside the better.

It is a relatively international dish, but the version I make is originally Serbian-Turkish "Sutlijas".

Very easy to make, you just need a bit of elbow grase ;).
