Przeno (translates to "fried")

Easy to cook, hard to perfect

This is a summer staple where I live, and I don't want to spend 1 summer without it.

Pros: very, veeery easy to make

Cons: very, veeery addictive. Also sadness when theres no fresh veggies to make it.

Ingredients

  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Spices

What you need to do:

  1. Cutting: prepare you dish with slicing both the peppers and tomatoes in smallish chunks
  2. Add a bit of olive oil in a pan/wok and wait for it to get hot
  3. Add just the peppers for now,salt and pepper them then wait for them to get softer and get a bit of golden colour
  4. When the peppers have been there for about 6-7-8 minutes you can add the tomatoes
  5. Once the tomatoes are in you can close the lid (or don't) and then be patient, it can take 15 minutes
  6. When you can no longer see a tomato slice, and theres just pepper and tomato sauce in the pan - turn the stove to low heat
  7. Last but not least: Add 2-3 eggs to the pan and whisk everything together furiously until theres no raw egg
  8. Done. Enjoy :)
